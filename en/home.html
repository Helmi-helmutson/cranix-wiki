CRANIX/CEPHALIX-Docs

The CRANIX Server is a modular open-source server solution based on Linux, designed for any environment where networks need to be managed centrally, securely, and efficiently. Whether for schools, businesses, public institutions, or other organizations, CRANIX provides a wide range of powerful features for administration, user management, communication, and digital services. Through the centralized management of users, devices, services, and networks, technical processes are simplified and administrative tasks are optimized in a sustainable way. Thanks to its consistent open-source approach, operators retain full control over their data, systems, and future development at all times. This ensures maximum independence, transparency, and long-term investment security for modern IT infrastructures. On this documentation site, you will find guides, information, and technical resources related to the installation, usage, administration, and development of CRANIX.
